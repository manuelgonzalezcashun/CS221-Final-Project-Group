

GAME RULES
    --- Player draws a value between 1-11
    --- Player draws until they reaches 21 or a value closest to 21

    WIN STATES:
        - Player wins if they get 21
	  - Oppenent gets a score over 21
	  - Player gets a score closest to 21, without going over 21
    LOSS STATES:
        - Player goes over 21
	  - Oppenent gets 21
	  - Oppenent gets a score closest to 21, without going over 21
